# 12 Most instructive DS Japanese softwares
#website/neosamurai

*THURSDAY, DECEMBER 21, 2006*

[12 Most instructive DS Japanese softwares](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://neosamurai.blogspot.com/2006/12/12-most-instructive-ds-japanese.html)

Can you read Japanese? You’d better spare your time and stop reading this entry if you don’t.
Do you own a Nintendo DS? You’d better read my  [entry on Japundit](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://japundit.com/archives/2006/12/21/4526/)  in the case you haven’t caught on the buzz. If these two conditions are fulfilled then consider yourself lucky, because you have got a good selection of games , or more correctly softs , to keep you entertained during the cold winter days. In Japan, game developers seem to be taking advantage of the huge success of Nintendo’s handheld, coming up with myriads of concepts and ideas catering to adult gamers. The games range from simple brain training programs, to cooking games, en passant by dictionaries, quizzes, and stock traders. What follows is actually my wishlist , but it is also a good reference for everyone willing to take full advantage of his/her DS this Christmas season:

カブトレ /Kabutore/: A stock trading simulation by the creator of Metal Gear Solid himself, Hideo Kojima. The game offers an opportunity to learn some basics of the Japanese stock exchange system. Check out  [Q-taro’s entry](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://blog.q-taro.com/archives/001484.php)  for further details. ( [Buy it from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/%E6%A0%AA%E5%BC%8F%E5%A3%B2%E8%B2%B7%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC%E3%83%BC%E3%83%8A%E3%83%BC-%E3%82%AB%E3%83%96%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC-%E7%89%B9%E5%85%B8-%E3%83%8D%E3%83%83%E3%83%88%E6%A0%AA%E5%88%9D%E5%BF%83%E8%80%85%E5%AE%8C%E5%85%A8%E3%82%AC%E3%82%A4%E3%83%89%E3%83%96%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF-179%E5%86%86%E3%81%AE%E3%82%AD%E3%83%A3%E3%83%83%E3%82%B7%E3%83%A5%E3%83%90%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF%E4%BB%98%E3%81%8D%E5%8F%A3%E5%BA%A7%E9%96%8B%E8%A8%AD%E7%94%B3%E8%BE%BC%E6%9B%B8%E5%B0%81%E5%85%A5/dp/B000IUCXMU/sr=11-1/qid=1166731947/ref=sr_11_1/503-0187066-4099922) )

こころを休める大人の塗り絵DS /Kokoro Wo Yasumeru Otona No Nurie DS:/A painting and coloring book where players stare at a colorful image and try to copy and recreate its colors on a monochrome clone of the target picture. Check out  [NCSX](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://ncsx.wordpress.com/2006/08/30/nintendo-ds-coloring-book/trackback/)  for further details. ( [Buy it from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000FISQNU/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP) )


200万人の漢検 (Nihyaku Banjin No Kanken): If you are an advanced learner of Kanji, this is the perfect software for you. Includes Kanji tests, quizzes, stroke orders, MCQs and a load of other features to help you improve your kanji writing and reading. a MUST-HAVE! ( [Buy it from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000HCFJHK/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP) )


漢字そのまま DS楽引辞典 Kanji Sono Mama DS Rakubiki Jiten: A Japanese-English dictionary with tons of features such as recognition handwritten Kanji , stroke order, quizzes… Conceived originally for Japanese people learning English, since it includes only English pronunciation, but it can be used in the other way around as well. ( [Get it from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000EPTZRK/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP) )


For those are advanced in their quest to master Nihongo, here are three softwares that may be of great help to strengthen grammar and lexicon:

 [正しい日本語DS](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000KHRCJU/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP)  Tadashii Nihongo DS

 [金田一先生の日本語レッスン](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000LAM8K4/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP)  Kindaichi No Nihongo Lesson

 [クイズ!日本語王](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000IUE2YC/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP)  Quiz! Nihongo Ou

DS時雨殿 DS Shigure Dono, a software that help players study and memorize Haiku poems and their accompanying artwork. Check out  [JapanProbes](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.japanprobe.com/?p=833)  entry for further details. ( [Buy it from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Nintendo-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E3%82%BF%E3%83%83%E3%83%81%E3%81%A7%E6%A5%BD%E3%81%97%E3%82%80%E7%99%BE%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%80%E9%A6%96/dp/B000KN69HA/sr=11-1/qid=1166789374/ref=sr_11_1/503-0187066-4099922) )

 [絶対音感トレーニング](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.success-corp.co.jp/software/ds/onkan/) DS /Zettai Onkan Toreiningu DS/; A musical game that offers several music theory lessons,beside pitch quizzes to test your musical skills. It features a beetle mascot that adds a fair amount of cuteness. The interface is in overall cute and colorful. ( [Buy from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/SUCCESS-Corporation-13306371-%E3%81%A6%E3%81%AE%E3%81%B2%E3%82%89%E6%A5%BD%E7%BF%92-%E7%B5%B6%E5%AF%BE%E9%9F%B3%E6%84%9F%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC%E3%83%BC%E3%83%8B%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B0DS/dp/B000IU8X2E/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_1/503-0187066-4099922) )

しゃべる!DSお料理ナビ Shaberu! DS O Ryouri Nabi: An excellent cookbook software that was a hit in Japan, selling more than 120,000 copies in its first week available. It features recipes of all kinds, with explanations, pictures, and everything you might need to prepare a delicious meal, well … except the real ingredients of course!
( [Buy from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Nintendo-Co-Ltd-%E3%81%97%E3%82%83%E3%81%B9%E3%82%8B-DS%E3%81%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86%E3%83%8A%E3%83%93/dp/B000ESH6DW/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_7/503-0187066-4099922) )

 [ご当地検定DS Gotouchi Kentei DS:](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.spike.co.jp/gotouchi/) You think that you are knowledgeable about Japanese prefectures, their local culture and customs? Here is the game that’ll make you discover that you ignored much about Japan. Obaasan will teach you plenty about Japanese prefectures, and test how far you grasped her rumblings. Features voice recognition, and manual character input.( [Buy from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Spike-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E3%81%94%E5%BD%93%E5%9C%B0%E6%A4%9C%E5%AE%9ADS/dp/B000JKT7CS/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_9/503-0187066-4099922) )


And here is the NUMBER ONE software on my wishlist….

## 日本の歴史DS

[日本の歴史DS](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://iek.on.arena.ne.jp/rankujunds/history.html)
Nihon No Rekishi DS (Japanese History DS)! A good educational software about Japanese history and Japanese historical figures ranging from Abe Seimei to Takeda Shingen,beside contemporary figures as well. A MUST HAVE, if you are into Japanese history! ( [Buy from Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Institute-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E5%AD%A6%E7%A0%94%E8%A6%81%E7%82%B9%E3%83%A9%E3%83%B3%E3%82%AF%E9%A0%86%E3%82%B7%E3%83%AA%E3%83%BC%E3%82%BA/dp/B000IZOTV8/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_12/503-0187066-4099922) )
