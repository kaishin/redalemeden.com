I'm still under the spell of the [Kikunokai](/blog/2006/06/kikunokai/)
performance, so here's a short historical overview.

Hata Michiyo, born in Kyoto, studied _Nihon Buyou_ (日本舞踊, Japanese
traditional dance) under Onoe Kikunojo, first master of the Onoe school of
dance, which was founded by the _kabuki_ actor Onoe Kikugoro. In 1972, she
founded Kikunokai, which has been collecting culture and arts awards ever since.

In 1976, the troupe received the Excellent Art Prize at the Cultural Festival
sponsored by the Cultural Agency of Japan. As a _Shihan_ (Master) of Onoe,
Hata-san has toured over 22 countries showcasing Japanese folkloric dance. She
is perhaps best known internationally for her choreography in Akira Kurosawa's
film _Dreams_.
