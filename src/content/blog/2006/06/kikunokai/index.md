After a week of stressful exams, I went to the
[Kikunokai show in Casablanca](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172634/http://www.moroccotimes.com/news/article.asp?id=15379)
at all costs, and I did.

[Kikunokai](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172634/http://www.kikunokai.co.jp/english.html)
is a traditional Japanese dance troupe founded by Michiyo Hata in 1972. Hata-san
studied dance under Onoe Kikunojo I, first master of the Onoe school, which was
founded by the _kabuki_ actor Onoe Kikugoro VI. The troupe performs classical
dances, _Taiko_ drums, chants, and musical comedies.

The show was fantastic, the _Taiko_ drums and _Awa-odori_ in particular. More
to come on this troupe.
