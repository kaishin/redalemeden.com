[Kiyomori](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172131/http://kiyomori.jp/main.html)
is a _samurai_-inspired robot by Tmusk, one of the first prototypes to walk in
a way that closely resembles human movement. After watching the video on their
site, it does seem genuinely remarkable, a step closer to
[Gundam](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172131/http://smt.blogs.com/mari_diary/2005/05/did_you_go_to_s.html).

Tmusk seems to be blending Japan's past and its future in one package.
