While browsing the archives of
[Mari's Watashi to Tokyo](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172136/http://smt.blogs.com/mari_diary/),
I came across a funny
[story about Hyde](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172136/http://www.typepad.com/t/trackback/4033827),
the vocalist of L'arc~en~ciel. It is rumored that Hyde (pronounced Haido) is
very short. He's never revealed his real height, but some 2-channel folks
assaulted his Wikipedia entry and scattered the rumored 156cm height all over
the article in increasingly absurd ways. The page was modified so many times it
ended up locked, like all controversial entries.

I checked
[the page history](https://web.archive.org/web/20061107172136/http://ja.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyde&diff=4167033&oldid=4167031)
and found some gems:

- 156hyde (ひゃくごじゅうろくハイド) は日本の身長156cm5'2"ヴォーカリスト —
  "156Hyde is Japan's 156cm (5'2") vocalist."
- 身長(実は156cm)、体重、生年月日等のプロフィールを完全に隠して…そして身長が56cmであることを公表した。 —
  "Height (truth is 156cm), body height and birth date are hidden…then he
  announced his height is 156cm."
- さらにはドラム、アルトサックスなど多様な楽器を身長156cmながら操ることができる。 —
  "Furthermore, even though he is 156cm, he can play various instruments
  including drums, alto sax…"
- 156年1月56日生まれ — "Born in the year 156, month 1, day 56."

They also listed his bands as:

- L'Arc〜en〜Ciel
- 156
- P'UNK～EN～CIEL
- HYDE
- 156センチ (centi)
- 国道156号線 (National Highway 156)
- 156cm
- Gackt
- ニルヴァーナ (Nirvana)
- メタリカ (Metallica)
- 百五十六 (one hundred fifty six)
- 山本五十六 (Yamamoto 56)
- 1.56m
- 1560mm
- ひゃくごじゅうろくはいど (156cm haido)

The English Wikipedia still has this info hidden between the lines. The French
wiki says 158cm, and the Japanese one gives a range from 156cm to 160cm,
emphasizing that Hyde has never announced it officially.
