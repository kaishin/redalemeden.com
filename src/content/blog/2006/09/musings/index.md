"I don't know how my story started, nor how it would unfold, even less how it
would end. Day after day, I feel like I don't belong to this world, that I was
born against the flow of time, in a wide Sahara desert where the horizon hides
the truth lying far beyond the burning sand dunes.

I have been walking, bare-foot, hoping to find the oasis…Only to get tricked by
the mirage over and over again…

'To where?' I uttered, kneeling on the blazing dunes.

My life has been this way since my very first cry, roaming aimlessly, with
nothing in hand but a gourd of despair. Having no landmarks to locate my weak
body, my quest to find a destination was all the more challenging. Until the day
I realized that the sun shines from the very same spot, every dawn, breaking the
chilling loneliness of the Sahara nights.

I couldn't help but get bewitched by the transcendental rising sun. I firmly
resolved to seek its rays, to bear hunger and thirst, and to keep on walking
under the daylight guidance.

I spend the nights counting the stars, hoping for the sun to rise again, to
light my pathway, thinking that no matter how long the night may get, a new
morning shall definitely come.

And thus goes on my story, seeking the sun, and panicking at the thought of
being blinded by its radiance."

_Kaishin, the lost samurai_
