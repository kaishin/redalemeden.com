After introducing the [Kojiki](/blog/2006/06/japanese-mythology-origins/) and
the [Nihonshoki](/blog/2006/06/the-nihonshoki/), the mythology itself: _Shinwa_
begins before Japan's creation, with deities appearing at the formation of the
universe (天地開闢 _Tenchikai Byaku_) on _Takama-no-hara_ (高天原), the High
Plain of Heaven.

The first three were called the Universe Three _Kami_ (造化の三神
_Zouka-no-sankami_):

- **Ameno-Minakanushi** (no-kami) 天之御中主神:
  Master-of-the-August-Centre-of-Heaven, associated with the North Star.
- **Takami-Musubi** (no-kami) 高御産巣日神: High-August-Producing-_Kami_.
- **Kami-Musubi** (no-kami) 神産巣日神: Divine-Producing-Wondrous-Deity.

Two more _Kami_ followed, born from a jellyfish-like form floating on the sea:

- **Umashi-ashikabi-hikoji** (no-kami) 宇摩志阿斯訶備比古遅神:
  Pleasant-Reed-Shoot-Prince-Elder.
- **Ameno-tokotachi** (no-kami) 天之常立神: Heavenly-Eternally-Standing-Deity.

These five disappeared with the creation of heaven and earth. Together they are
called _Koto-amatsu-gami_ (別天津神), the Distinguishing Heavenly _Kami_, who
were born alone and hid themselves.

After the _Koto-amatsu-gami_ came the _Kami-noyo-nana-yo_ (神世七代), the Seven
_Kami_ Generations: 10 _Kami_ and _Megami_ in 7 generations, with the last five
each pairing a _Kami_ and a _Megami_:

- **Kunino-tokotachi** (no-kami) 国之常立神: Earthly-Eternally-Standing-Deity.
- **Toyokuninushi** (no-kami) 豊雲野神: Luxuriant-Integrating-Master.
- **Uiji** (no-kami) 宇比邇神: Mud-Earth-Lord · **Suiji** (no-kami) 須比智邇神:
  Mud-Earth-Lady.
- **Tsumegui** (no-kami) 角杙神: Germ-Integrating-Deity · **Ikugui**
  (no-kami) 活杙神: Life-Integrating-Deity.
- **Ooto-noji** (no-kami) 意富斗能地神: Elder-of-the-Great-Place · **Ooto-nobe**
  (no-kami) 大斗乃弁神: Elder-Lady-of-the-Great-Place.
- **Omodaru** (no-kami) 淤母陀琉神: Perfect-Exterior · **Ayakashi-kone**
  (no-kami) 阿夜訶志古泥神: Oh-Awful-Lady.
- **Izanagi** (no-kami) 伊邪那岐神: Male-who-Invites · **Izanami**
  (no-kami) 伊邪那美神: Female-who-Invites.

The relationship between the five last-generation pairs is elder brother and
younger sister (_Kyoudai_ 兄妹). Izanagi and Izanami, the final pair, were
commanded by the _Koto-amatsu-gami_ to give form to the drifting land; they are
the subject of the next entry.

Glossary: _Shinwa_ = Japanese mythology · _Kami_ = deity · _Megami_ = goddess
