Izanagi fled [Yomi](/blog/2006/09/shinwa-realm-of-hades/), the land of Hades,
and resolved to purify himself: "Nay! hideous! I have come to a hideous and
polluted land, So I will perform the purification of my person."

He headed to **Awakihara** (阿波岐原, the Valley of Japanese Clovers) and
undressed beside a river. As in all _Shinwa_ events, _Kami_ were born from each
item he shed, bringing the total count of deities to over 8,000. Twelve deities
were born from his garb:

- From his staff: **Tsukitatsu-funato** (no-kami) 衝立船戸神:
  Thrust-Stand-Come-No-Further.
- From his _obi_ sash: **Michino-nagachiha** (no-kami) 道之長乳歯神:
  Road-Long-Space.
- From his bag: **Tokiha-kashi** (no-kami) 時量師神: Loosen-Put.
- From his clothes: **Wazurahi-noushi** (no-kami) 和豆良比能宇斯能神:
  Master-of-Trouble.
- From his _hakama_: **Michimata** (no-kami) 道俣神: Road-Fork-Deity.
- From his crown: **Akigu-hinoushi** (no-kami) 飽咋之宇斯能神:
  Master-of-the-Open-Mouth.
- From his left bracelet: **Oki-zakaru** (no-kami) 奥疎神 (Shore-Distant),
  **Okutsu-nagisabiko** (no-kami) 奥津那芸佐毘古神 (Wash-Prince-of-the-Shore),
  **Okitsuka-hibera**
  (no-kami) 奥津甲斐弁羅神 (Intermediate-Direction-of-the-Shore).
- From his right bracelet: **Hezakaru** (no-kami) 辺疎神 (Offing-Distant),
  **Hetsunagi-sabiko** (no-kami) 辺津那芸佐毘古神 (Wash-Prince-of-the-Offing),
  **Hetsuka-hibera**
  (no-kami) 辺津甲斐弁羅神 (Intermediate-Direction-of-the-Offing).

Izanagi then entered the river at mid-stream, finding the upper waters too swift
and the lower too sluggish. From the filth washed from his body two evil deities
were born: **Yasoma-gatsuhi**
(no-kami) 八十禍津日神 (Wondrous-Deity-of-Eighty-Evils) and **Ohoma-gatsuhi**
(no-kami) 大禍津日神 (Wondrous-Deity-of-Great-Evils). To counter them, three
rectifying _Kami_ emerged: **Kamu-naobi** (no-kami) 神直毘神, **Oho-naobi**
(no-kami) 大直毘神, and **Izunome** 伊豆能売.

As he bathed at each level of the river, more sea deities were born:

- Bottom water: **Sokotsu-watatsumi** (no-kami) 底津綿津見神 and
  **Sokotsu-tsunowo** (no-kami) 底筒之男神.
- Mid water: **Nakatsu-watatsumi** (no-kami) 中津綿津見神 and
  **Nakatsu-tsunowo** (no-kami) 中筒之男神.
- Surface water: **Uhatsu-watatsumi** (no-kami) 上津綿津見神 and
  **Uhatsu-tsunowo** (no-kami) 上筒之男神.

Finally, when Izanagi washed his left eye, **Amaterasu-ohomi**
(kami) 天照大御神 (Heaven-Shining-Great-August-Deity) was born. From his right
eye came **Tsukiyomi-no-mikoto** 月読命 (Moon-Night-Possessor). And from washing
his nose came **Takehaya-susanowo-nomikoto**
建速須佐之男命 (Brave-Swift-Impetuous-Male-Augustness).

These three, known as **Amaterasu** (the sun), **Tsukiyomi** (the moon), and
**Susanowo** (wind and storm), are the most important deities in _Shinwa_ and
will be the focus of subsequent entries.
