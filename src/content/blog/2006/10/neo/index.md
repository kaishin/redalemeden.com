"Neo" is a prefix derived from Greek for "new," used whenever there is an urge
to convey the freshness of something old that has been revived. It shows up with
unprecedented frequency across politics, arts, and just about everything else.
Handy, but slightly worrying. Refreshing, but lacking originality. And as
humanity advances, what comes next? Neo Neo? Ultra Neo?

Until then, this blog will be called Neo Samurai.
