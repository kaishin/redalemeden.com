After
[the purification of Izanagi](/blog/2006/09/shinwa-purification-of-izanagi/),
Izanagi assigned the three children their domains: Amaterasu to rule
_Takamanohara_ (the High Plain of Heaven), Tsukiyomi to rule the night, and
Susanowo to rule the sea. The first two fulfilled their roles, but Susanowo only
wept and lamented, sobbing until rivers dried and mountains withered.

Izanagi demanded an explanation. Susanowo replied: "I wail because I wish to
depart to my deceased mother's land, to the Nether Distant Land." Izanagi,
furious, expelled him.

Before descending, Susanowo resolved to bid farewell to his sister Amaterasu.
His approach was so noisy and turbulent that Amaterasu, suspecting ill intent,
armed herself: she tied her hair in two threads adorned with jewels, strapped on
two quivers, grasped her bow, and stood ready for battle. Susanowo assured her:
"I have no evil intent. It is only that I wish to take leave of thee and
depart."

"If that be so, whereby shall I know the sincerity of thine intentions?" she
asked.

Susanowo proposed a trial: each would produce children from the other's
possessions. The sex of the offspring would prove intent. Amaterasu took
Susanowo's sword **Totsuka-no-tsurugi** and broke it into three pieces. She
washed them in the heavenly well, chewed them, and blew out her breath, giving
birth to three _Megami_:

- **Takiribime** 多紀理毘売命: Torrent-Mist-Princess.
- **Ichikishima-hime** 市寸島比売命: Princess-of-the-Island-of-the-Offing.
- **Takitsuhime** 多岐都比売命: Lovely-Island-Princess.

Then Susanowo took the 500 Yasaka jewels from Amaterasu's hair and wrists,
washed them, chewed them, and blew out his breath, giving birth to five _Kami_:

- From the jewels on her left hair-bunch:
  **Masakatsu-akatsu-kachihayabi-ameno-oshiomimi** 正勝吾勝勝速日天之忍穂耳命:
  Truly-Conquering-Swift-Heavenly-Great-Ears.
- From the right hair-bunch: **Amenohohi** 天之菩卑能命.
- From her head-dress: **Amashi-hikone** 天津日子根命: Prince-Lord-of-Heaven.
- From her left wrist: **Ikushihi-kone** 活津日子根命: Prince-Lord-of-Life.
- From her right wrist: **Kuma-no-kusubi** 熊野久須毘命.

Amaterasu declared the three female _Kami_ Susanowo's children (born from his
sword) and the five male _Kami_ her own (born from her jewels), judging this as
proof of his pure intentions. The consequences of that declaration would unfold
in the next entry.
