A brief summary of the first six _Shinwa_ entries, for anyone following along.

Before Japan's creation, pure elements formed the heavens and murky elements the
earth. Five _Kami_ appeared spontaneously and then vanished. Seven _Kami_
generations followed, the last pair being Izanagi and Izanami, a brother and
sister commanded by the heavenly _Kami_ to create the land.

Standing on the Floating Bridge of Heaven, they stirred the sea with a sacred
spear until an island formed. They descended, built a palace, and performed the
divine union that gave birth to the Japanese archipelago and several deities.
Izanami bore Kagutsushi, the fire deity, whose birth burned her and killed her.
She was sent to _Yomi_, the underground realm of the dead.

Izanagi could not accept the separation. He slew Kagutsushi and descended to
_Yomi_ to bring Izanami back. He found her body rotting in the darkness, fled in
horror, and barely escaped the demons she sent after him. He blocked the
entrance to _Yomi_ with a great boulder, separating the living from the dead. He
then purified himself in a river, and from that act of cleansing were born
Amaterasu (the sun), Tsukiyomi (the moon), and Susanowo (wind and storm).
