If going around the globe with your canvas and watercolours is your hobby, then
there is someone from whom you can learn a lot. Nishikawa-san is an
over-60-year-old Japanese artist who tours the world with his "F-1 size sketch
book and a set of water paints in the pocket of his jacket", as he stated on his
English webpage. Nishikawa-san's sketches are interesting in the way they
picture the scenery at a given moment from his very own point of view. Even
though they are not as detailed as a photograph, they are full of life. Unlike
taking photos, which ends as soon as the shutter is pressed, sketches allow the
person to enjoy and interact with the scene even more. New York, England, Peru,
France, China, South Korea were but a few of the many destinations depicted in
Nishikawa-san's sketches.
