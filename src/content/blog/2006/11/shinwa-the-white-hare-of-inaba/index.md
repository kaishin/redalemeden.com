After having wedded princess _Kushinada_ and bore several children, _Susanowo_
headed to _Yomi_, as he planned since he was expelled to earth.

Time has elapsed, and generations have succeeded. Nothing notable happened until
a valiant descendant of _Susanowo_ and his myriads of older brothers decided to
go on a long trip seeking princess _Yagami_. _Oonamuji_ (大国主) and his eighty
older brothers left _Izumo_ towards _Inaba_, where the beautiful princess
dwells. Being the youngest, he was overpowered by his brothers, and thus was
forced to carry their luggage during the journey. _Oonamuji_ grew tired and
straggled behind his brothers. When he reached Cape _Keta_, he found a badly
wounded white hare. The creature's fur was pulled out, and the bruises on its
naked skin were directly exposed to the burning sun rays. _Oonamuji_ drew nearer
to peruse the hare and enquire about its outcome, "Why liest thou weeping?". The
worn out creature explained that he was in the island of _Oki_ (not too far from
_Izumo_ and _Inaba_) and had no way to cross the sea to reach the mainland, so
he thought of a deceitful scheme. He called out one shark (crocodile of the sea
as in Hall Chamberlain's translation) and challenged him that the hares community
is larger than the sharks one. The white hare asked the shark to summon all his
friends so that HE can count them to prove the supremacy of his own community.
Tempted, the shark called out all his companions, and swam on the surface
alongside each other. The hare jumped on their backs, as if he was counting
them. But his real purpose was to cross the sea on the bridge made by the
sharks. However, he couldn't control his excitement, and said when he jumped
over the last shark "You have been deceived by me!". The sharks went furious
against him when he unveiled his real intentions, and assaulted him violently.
He then lied on the ground for hours, until he was discovered by the eighty
brothers who were ahead of _Oonamuji_. He told them his story and asked for
advice. "Bathe in the salt water, and lie down exposed to the wind under the
sun." was their proposal. He followed their words, only to increase the pain and
suffering...

_Oonamuji_ passed by the injured creature and asked the same question. The poor
hare repeated his tragic story. _Oonamuji_ gave him different instructions:

"Go quickly now to the river, wash thy body with the fresh water, then take the
pollen of the sedges growing at the river-mouth, spread it about, and roll about
upon it, then hide in the shade; whereupon thy body will certainly be restored
to its original state." Instructed _Oonamuji_.

The white hare of _Inaba_ did as he was told, and his body restored to its
original state. The creature promised _Oonamuji_ that the princess will choose
him for his gentle heart.
