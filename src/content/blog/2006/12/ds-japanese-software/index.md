If you can read Japanese and own a Nintendo DS, Japanese developers have created educational software ranging from brain training to dictionaries to stock trading simulations. Here's my wishlist:

**カブトレ (Kabutore):** Stock trading simulation by Hideo Kojima teaching basics of the Japanese stock exchange. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/%E6%A0%AA%E5%BC%8F%E5%A3%B2%E8%B2%B7%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC%E3%83%BC%E3%83%8A%E3%83%BC-%E3%82%AB%E3%83%96%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC-%E7%89%B9%E5%85%B8-%E3%83%8D%E3%83%83%E3%83%88%E6%A0%AA%E5%88%9D%E5%BF%83%E8%80%85%E5%AE%8C%E5%85%A8%E3%82%AC%E3%82%A4%E3%83%89%E3%83%96%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF-179%E5%86%86%E3%81%AE%E3%82%AD%E3%83%A3%E3%83%83%E3%82%B7%E3%83%A5%E3%83%90%E3%83%83%E3%82%AF%E4%BB%98%E3%81%8D%E5%8F%A3%E5%BA%A7%E9%96%8B%E8%A8%AD%E7%94%B3%E8%BE%BC%E6%9B%B8%E5%B0%81%E5%85%A5/dp/B000IUCXMU/sr=11-1/qid=1166731947/ref=sr_11_1/503-0187066-4099922))

**こころを休める大人の塗り絵DS:** Coloring book where players recreate colors on monochrome images. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000FISQNU/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP))

**200万人の漢検 (Nihyaku Banjin No Kanken):** Comprehensive kanji learning software with tests, quizzes, and stroke orders. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000HCFJHK/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP))

**漢字そのまま DS楽引辞典:** Japanese-English dictionary with handwritten kanji recognition. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000EPTZRK/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP))

**Grammar and vocabulary:** [正しい日本語DS](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000KHRCJU/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP), [金田一先生の日本語レッスン](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000LAM8K4/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP), [クイズ!日本語王](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/switch-language/product/B000IUE2YC/ref=dp_change_lang/503-0187066-4099922?ie=UTF8&language=en%5FJP)

**DS時雨殿:** Study haiku poems with accompanying artwork. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Nintendo-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E3%82%BF%E3%83%83%E3%83%81%E3%81%A7%E6%A5%BD%E3%81%97%E3%82%80%E7%99%BE%E4%BA%BA%E4%B8%80%E9%A6%96/dp/B000KN69HA/sr=11-1/qid=1166789374/ref=sr_11_1/503-0187066-4099922))

**[絶対音感トレーニング](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.success-corp.co.jp/software/ds/onkan/):** Music theory lessons and pitch quizzes. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/SUCCESS-Corporation-13306371-%E3%81%A6%E3%81%AE%E3%81%B2%E3%82%89%E6%A5%BD%E7%BF%92-%E7%B5%B6%E5%AF%BE%E9%9F%B3%E6%84%9F%E3%83%88%E3%83%AC%E3%83%BC%E3%83%8B%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B0DS/dp/B000IU8X2E/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_1/503-0187066-4099922))

**しゃべる!DSお料理ナビ:** Cookbook software with recipes and cooking instructions. Sold 120,000 copies in its first week. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Nintendo-Co-Ltd-%E3%81%97%E3%82%83%E3%81%B9%E3%82%8B-DS%E3%81%8A%E6%96%99%E7%90%86%E3%83%8A%E3%83%93/dp/B000ESH6DW/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_7/503-0187066-4099922))

**[ご当地検定DS:](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.spike.co.jp/gotouchi/)** Quiz game about Japanese prefectures with voice recognition. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Spike-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E3%81%94%E5%BD%93%E5%9C%B0%E6%A4%9C%E5%AE%9ADS/dp/B000JKT7CS/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_9/503-0187066-4099922))

**[日本の歴史DS:](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://iek.on.arena.ne.jp/rankujunds/history.html)** Japanese history covering figures from Abe Seimei to Takeda Shingen. ([Amazon.jp](https://web.archive.org/web/20070518233003/http://www.amazon.co.jp/Institute-Co-Ltd-13306371-%E5%AD%A6%E7%A0%94%E8%A6%81%E7%82%B9%E3%83%A9%E3%83%B3%E3%82%AF%E9%A0%86%E3%82%B7%E3%83%AA%E3%83%BC%E3%82%BA/dp/B000IZOTV8/ref=cm_lm_fullview_prod_12/503-0187066-4099922))
