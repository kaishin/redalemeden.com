My first post in 2007. It took me a week to spare some time for blogging, due to
the fact that I am having my mid-term exams. As for 2007 Neo Samurai
resolutions, I have made up my mind to invest more time and effort in my blog.
It's not like it'll reap me fame nor anything, but it's a good way to make new
acquaintances and speak up your ideas.

Furthermore, I am willing to make a schedule for my posts, in the form of
thematic weekly columns. This way visitors will know beforehand what to expect
from the entry. The themes will be as follows: Mythology, History, Indie music,
Japanese culture for newbies, Funny tidbits, Japan around the web and a weekly
entry about myself. This structure will help me find enough content to write
about everyday, or at least once every two days. If any of the following topics
may interest you, please check every now and then.
