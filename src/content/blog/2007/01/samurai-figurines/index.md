I have mentioned
[Cube Works](https://web.archive.org/web/20070519053555/http://www.cube-works.co.jp/works/index.html)
briefly in my pink entry, a Japanese company that makes various products,
ranging from stuffed animals, to key chains and figurines. While browsing their
website tonight, I came across an
[interesting collection](https://web.archive.org/web/20070519053555/http://www.cube-works.co.jp/works/index_sub.html?/works/historylnquiry/index.html)
of historical figures figurines.

Each figurine measures about 10cm in height, and costs ¥504. I'd grab'em all if
I had the chance!
