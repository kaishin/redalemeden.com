光陰矢のごとし (_Kouin ya no gotoshi_). 光陰 (_Kouin_) is an old synonym
of 時間 (_Jikan_), which means time. It is written as a compound of two Kanji,
the first is 光 (_Hikari_) meaning light, and the second is 陰 (_Kage_), which
stands for shadow.

矢 (_Ya_) means arrow and ごとし (_Gotoshi_) means "like" or "as if", usually
written as 如し.

This is enough to figure out the meaning of this _Kotowaza_ I guess.
